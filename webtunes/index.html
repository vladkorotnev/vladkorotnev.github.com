What is WebTunes?

WebTunes is a music library webapp that allows you to listen to music wherever you go! You just need a computer with a modern web browser and an internet connection. Log in with your password â€” and you're good to go!

Multiformat. Don't bother converting your music.

Easy-to-use. No need for manuals.

The WebTunes GUI is similar to the one all other music player have, so you are most likely to know how to use it already.

No installation required. Listen anywhere.

What about PortaTune?

Whereas WebTunes might be a nice thing for use on desktop, it is really hard to use such a UI on a small touchscreen. So PortaTune is aimed to help you carry your music everywhere you go, even without a computer.

Easy to set up. Tap to tune in.

Remember those radio stations you've entered in WebTunes? Well, you can listen to them from PortaTune too!

Pretty much, that's it.
